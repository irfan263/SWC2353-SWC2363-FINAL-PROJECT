Welcome to Podcasterk

Welcome to Podcasterk, Sterk Production is a group of young Malaysians who manage their own YouTube channel. They consist of five young men: Farhan Mazlan, Fikri Latif, Harith Zazman, Rashdan Samsudin, and Mirol Sabirin. Sterk Production has shown impressive performance, enabling them to hire two video editors and have two interns. They were also listed as one of the top 5 influencers at the MeleTOP Era 2020 Awards in the Influencer Meletop Syok category.

Whether you're a seasoned podcaster or just starting out, our goal is to provide you with valuable content to enhance your podcasting journey. From tips and tricks to industry trends, we've got you covered.
